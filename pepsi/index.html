History

In 1893, Caleb Bradham, a young pharmacist from New Bern, North Carolina,
begins experimenting with different soft drink mixtures.
Customers and friends sample his drugstore sodas.
One of Caleb's formulations, known as "Brad's Drink" in 1898,
was renamed "Pepsi-Cola" on August 28, 1898. PepsiCo receives its first logo.
Founded the first Pepsi-Cola Company in 1902.

And today, PEPSI celebrates its 125th anniversary.
Pepsi is updating its brand identity with the introduction of a new logo after 14 years.
The latest logo from the soft drink company celebrates its 125 anniversary.

Nitro pepsi

Introducing Nitro Pepsi,
the first nitrogen-infused cola.
It's more than just a cola, it's an experience.

A fascinating waterfall created by small bubbles.
Smooth and creamy texture from the first sip.

  • Why it exists

    Regular soda isn’t for everyone.
    It can be heavily carbonated
    and acidic.
    Nitro beverages may be more approachable.

  • flavors

    Nitro Pepsi is available in two delicious flavors:
    Draft Cola and Vanilla Draft Cola.
    Some people even find our drinks taste like vanilla ice cream.

  • texture

    Expect foam, bubbles,
    and softness all at the same time.
    Think of the thick sea foam
    found on beaches right where
    the water touches the sand.

  • chill can

    Open the can to hear
    the distinct sound of smooth,
    nitrogen-infused bubbles
    serenading your ears
    and overwhelming your senses.

  • pour hard

    Pour hard into a glass
    to experience a beautiful
    cascading effect as
    the tiny bubbles race around.

  • admire&enjoy!

    Enjoy the fantastic
    soft foam with your eyes
    and feel it with your mouth!

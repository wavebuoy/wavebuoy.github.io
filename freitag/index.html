About

Our Journey
to circularity.

We think and act in cycles
it’s been freitag’s corporate philosophy for more than 25 years now.

Today,
we’re thinking mainly about how we can act even better in closed cycles.
Something that started with the recycling of discarded
truck tarps has encouraged us time
and time again to use resources and energy sparingly.
For us, the transition from a linear, disposable society to a circular
economy where resources are no longer wasted is a top priority.

  • The production of freitag bags

    From truck
    till bag.

    The recycling of used trucks is changed to unique.

    The process of recycling takes place
    in very complex stages in our factory.

    • Step Ⅰ

    • Step Ⅱ

    • Step Ⅴ

    • Truckspotting

      It may not be Glencore.
      But sourcing the raw materials freitag
      needs is still pretty hard-core.

    • Cutting up the tarps

      Freeing tarps from their incarnation as dirty,
      bulky truck covers so they can be processed
      further is no walk in the park.

    • Washing

      What happens in the basement at the Nœrd has nothing
      in common with what you do in your laundry room –
      even though it might look almost the same.

    • Bag design

      The bag designers ensure that our one-of-a-kind
      products are not just unique but uniquely beautiful as well.

    • Completion

      At the freitag factory, we only sew prototypes and test bags.
      Everything else – including your freitag bag – is entrusted to
      our experienced longtime partners,
      who are committed to complying with our Code of Conduct.
